About

Recently, I received the Ph.D. degree in the School of Automation, Central South University (CSU), Changsha, China. I visited the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Victoria (UVic), Victoria, BC, Canada.

For me, research is not only a means of acquiring wisdom but also a way to impart knowledge and motivate people, which I find deeply fulfilling.

Research

My current research projects and interests include:
