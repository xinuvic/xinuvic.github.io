About

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in the School of Automation, Central South University, Changsha, China, and a visiting Ph.D. student with the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of Victoria, Victoria, BC, Canada.

#

I enjoy every aspect of research, from learning/creating new knowledge to building collaborations and observing the confidence it brings to people.

For me, research is not only a means of acquiring wisdom but also a way to impart knowledge and motivate people, which I find deeply fulfilling.

Research

My current research projects and interests include:

  • Cellular vehicle-to-everything (C-V2X)
  • Wireless resource management
  • Protocol design
